BeingVaidya

A unified platform for Ayurveda doctors & students

Flutter Firebase GenAI

01 The Problem

There was no reliable platform for Ayurveda doctors and students to access trustworthy information, study materials, and productivity tools — all in one place. The existing resources were scattered, outdated, or simply not designed for the Ayurveda community. Students struggled to find quality content, and practitioners had no centralized hub to stay updated or improve their workflow.

02 The Solution

We built BeingVaidya — a unified platform that brings together everything an Ayurveda professional needs. From reliable study materials and reference tools to job listings, events, and practitioner resources, the platform covers the full spectrum of needs for both students and working doctors.

  • Curated, reliable Ayurveda study materials and references
  • Job listings and event updates for the Ayurveda community
  • Practitioner tools to boost daily productivity
  • GenAI-powered features for smarter content discovery

03 Tech Stack

  • Flutter for cross-platform mobile development
  • Firebase for backend, auth, and real-time data
  • GenAI integration for intelligent content features
