FutureWire

Your daily dose of curated tech & finance news

Web GenAI Automation

01 The Problem

I wanted to stay on top of the latest tech and finance news without manually browsing dozens of sources every day. Google News is great, but I wanted content curated to my specific interests — delivered in a clean, blog-style format that I could scan quickly each morning.

02 The Solution

Built FutureWire as a fully automated AI-powered blog. It pulls top stories from Google News related to tech and finance, processes them through AI to generate clean blog-style articles, and publishes them daily — zero manual effort required.

  • Automated daily scraping of top tech & finance news from Google
  • AI-powered article generation for clean, readable blog posts
  • Fully hands-off — runs on autopilot every single day
  • Personalized to my interest areas in technology and finance
  • Built this for myself, but it's publicly accessible

03 Tech Stack

  • Web scraping and automation for daily content pipeline
  • GenAI for transforming news into blog-style articles
  • Automated deployment and scheduling for zero-touch operation
