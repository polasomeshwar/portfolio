Sproutly

A habit-building app with a twist — your avatar depends on you

Mobile Cloud

01 The Problem

Most habit trackers are boring. They show you checkboxes and streaks, but there's no emotional connection — nothing that makes you genuinely care about missing a day. People download them, use them for a week, and then forget they exist. The usual approach of graphs and stats doesn't create the kind of attachment needed to build lasting habits.

02 The Solution

Sproutly takes a completely different approach to habit tracking. Instead of charts, you get a virtual avatar — a character that thrives when you follow your habits and visibly shrinks or loses its charm when you don't. It creates an emotional hook that makes skipping a habit feel personal.

  • Virtual avatar that grows and evolves as you maintain habits
  • Avatar visibly deteriorates when habits are skipped — creating urgency
  • Emotional attachment drives consistency better than graphs
  • Automated scheduling and smart notifications
  • Something genuinely different from the usual habit tracker formula

03 Tech Stack

  • Mobile development for native app experience
  • Cloud infrastructure for user data and avatar state sync
  • Push notification system for habit reminders
