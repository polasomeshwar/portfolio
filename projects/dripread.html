DripRead

Build a reading habit, one drip at a time

App Dev GenAI Python

01 The Problem

Most people want to start reading books but give up after just 1-2 days. The commitment of finishing an entire book feels overwhelming, and without a system to stay consistent, the habit never sticks. Traditional reading apps don't solve the consistency problem — they just give you the full book and expect you to figure it out.

02 The Solution

DripRead breaks the reading barrier by delivering bite-sized book content on a schedule. Instead of facing a 300-page book, you get a manageable 5-10 page summary delivered daily at your chosen time — like a drip feed for your brain.

  • Daily summaries of 5-10 pages sent at user-scheduled times
  • Continue reading the full book from where the summary left off
  • AI-powered summaries using Gemini API for intelligent compression
  • PDF and EPUB parsing for any book format
  • Progress tracking to visualize your reading streak

03 Tech Stack

  • Mobile app development for the client experience
  • Python backend for PDF/EPUB parsing and processing
  • Gemini API integration for AI-generated summaries
  • Scheduled notification system for daily delivery
